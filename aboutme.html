ABOUT ME

UX/UI
Designer

With 3 years of experience in a product-based environment. I’ve had the opportunity to participated in end-to-end product development — from business analysis and userflow design to UI implementation, testing, and UX enhancement.

With a mindset of continuous improvement and always putting my heart into every design I believe that I can bring practical value to any business I work with.

I'm Nga Pham

I was born and raised in Ho Chi Minh City and hold a bachelor’s degree in International Business Administration. After graduating and working for a year, I decided to switch paths to pursue my passion for Design, which had been with me since my school days. I completed the DIM program at Arena Multimedia and currently have three years of experience working in a product-based environment.
Starting later than other young colleagues has become the motivation for me to constantly strive and work hard, so as not to waste the remaining time. Above all, I always feel grateful for my family who has always accompanied and supported me on this important journey.
